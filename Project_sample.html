FALL

Simulation of a journey about finding the emotions, which fell apart after break up, one by one to find how to love again.



Artist: Njinx

Engine used: Unity

Date: 2023.12.18

Time Spent: 1 month

<- About

This story is based on my friend's experience.



After break up, you lost every emotions you had and became void.

Then you started writing your diaries about what you feel.

As time pasts, you slowly find you emotions one by one.



The memories you undergo, the behaviors you did all affects your emotions.



Spacial Texts (Picture 1)
reflects your thoughts.

Memorial Points (Picture 2)
rewinds your memory.

Diary Notes (Picture 3)
directly shows how you felt.
